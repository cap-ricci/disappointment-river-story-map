Get started

Hello there! Ready to discover the 18th century arctic exploration? Your control center is located on the left. You can choose between two paths, Storymaps and Timeline:

  • Storymaps let you follow one storyline at a time, let it be an exploration expedition or life facts about the explorers. We recommend you start here.
  • Timeline gives a more comprehensive approach, showing all storymaps at once. This way we think that you can better appreaciate the spatiotemporal distribution of the events.
But that's not the end of the story! You can also
  • Choose different base maps
  • Add or remove data layers, great to learn more about the surroundings!
And the cherry on the cake: the Ice timeline!
Ready for a completely different persective? Use the icon to get information about each controller.
You can always come back here by clicking the big ? in the top navbar. Enjoy!
